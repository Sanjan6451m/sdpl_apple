Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)

Apple Macintosh may have a higher initial investment compared to Windows PCs, but they offer a significantly lower Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) over time thanks to fewer support issues, longer hardware longevity, and higher resale value. Enterprises benefit from reduced IT overhead, improved employee satisfaction, and enhanced security, with macOS updates and Apple's suite of productivity and creativity apps—like Preview, Pages, Numbers, Keynote and iMovie—provided at no additional cost. Adopting Macs helps organisations streamline operations, empower teams, and gain long-term value from their technology investments.
