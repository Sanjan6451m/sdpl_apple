About Customer

One of the leading lens manufacturers in the world of vision technology, This organisation is the world’s largest private optical and ophthalmic research centre, with over 170 years of research and more than 430 scientists, positioning them as a leader in optical innovation.

Business Challenge

Sales and Marketing team faced the challenge of needing a seamless solution to enhance their product presentations, streamline the sharing of product catalogues, and efficiently generate sales orders. The desired outcome was to modernize their sales operations for improved efficiency and customer engagement.

Approach

To address the business challenge, this company decided to equip their Sales and Marketing team with advanced mobile technology to transform their traditional sales processes.



Usage

The company deployed over 400 iPads. These devices were utilized by the Sales and Marketing team to showcase product portfolios and facilitate real-time sales order processing.

Outcomes

The adoption of iPads revolutionized the way this company's Sales and Marketing team operates. This digital transformation is highlighted as important for modernizing sales operations, ultimately enhancing customer engagement and business outcomes.

Apple and Partner solutions demonstrated

Superior Digital enabled Essilor’s digital transformation by supplying 400 iPads and implementing a robust MDM solution to manage devices remotely and distribute product manuals, fact sheets, and business documents seamlessly. We also empowered their content creation workflow by providing Macs to design engaging eBooks for efficient distribution across the iPad fleet.
