Communications Guide

Overview

Developers choose Mac for an amazing range of tasks, whether they’re capturing initial ideas, designing wireframes, writing code, compiling builds, or connecting to backend systems.

This document is designed to help Apple Authorized Resellers accurately describe Mac as a powerful device for developers, across the many things they do each day. Use the headlines and copy blocks provided in your marketing materials.

All content is subject to the terms and conditions of your Apple reseller agreement. For guidance on using the Apple brand and Apple trademarks, please refer to the Apple Identity Guidelines. You can also contact your Apple account manager for guidance. For more information about Mac, visit apple.com/mac.
