About Customer

This Institute Technology is an educational institution committed to addressing global socio-economic needs through quality education and innovation.

Business Challenge

The primary challenge of this institution was to equip students with the necessary skills and hands-on experience to excel in mobile app development. Limited access to advanced technological tools and platforms was hindering students' ability to practice and innovate in the rapidly evolving tech landscape. The desired outcome was to foster learning in app development among students.



Approach

To overcome this challenge, the institute decided to establish a dedicated Apple Lab, providing students with a conducive environment and high- quality resources to learn app development effectively.

Usage

The institution set up and configured a state-of-the-art Apple Lab equipped with 30 iMacs, along with test iPads and iPhones. This lab provided students with the tools needed to learn, develop, and test mobile applications.



Outcomes

The establishment of the MacLab led to a significant increase in student engagement and productivity. Students were able to develop and test mobile applications efficiently, resulting in numerous successful projects and prototypes. The hands-on learning approach empowered students with practical experience and boosted their confidence in app development, preparing them for the demands of the global tech industry.

Apple and Partner solutions demonstrated

Superior Digital successfully equipped this technology institute with a student lab of 30 iMacs, tailored for training in App Development and Content Creation using Apple apps, developer tools, and specialized third-party solutions.
