iPhone Deployment

About Customer

A company dedicated to enhancing healthcare accessibility by integrating technology with healthcare to combat vision impairment. Their mission is to prevent blindness, preserve vision, and impact a billion lives through innovative and user-friendly solutions, focusing on early detection of eye diseases using advanced technology.

Business Challenge

This organisation's primary challenge was to improve healthcare service delivery by integrating high-quality technological solutions that are both reliable and user-friendly. They needed devices capable of supporting their advanced medical applications, such as internal fixation and auto capture, and facilitating efficient patient data management.



Approach

To meet these challenges, this organisation chose to integrate Apple's mobile technology into their medical solutions, leveraging its reputation for quality, reliability, and user-friendliness.

Usage

They integrated over 2,000 iPhones into their medical solutions. These iPhones serve as key components in their Anterior Imaging Module, supporting 4K quality video recording and live broadcasts essential for accurate diagnosis and collaborative medical procedures. The iPhones also enabled seamless patient data management and served as a robust platform for a Microscope Recording Device.



Outcomes

The deployment of iPhones ensured consistent performance with seamless post-sales support, guaranteeing uninterrupted operation and reliability for healthcare professionals. This significantly enhanced their solution capabilities, facilitating early detection of eye diseases and contributing to blindness prevention. The technology's ease of use led to wide adoption, supporting the organisation's mission to touch a billion lives.

Apple and Partner solutions demonstrated

Superior Digital successfully supplied 2,000 iPhones to this organisation, a leader in medical technology, ensuring seamless integration with their proprietary applications and diagnostic equipment. Along with the devices, we delivered essential accessories, configuration, and deployment services to enable a fully functional and ready-to-use mobility solution.
