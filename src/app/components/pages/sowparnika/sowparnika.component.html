Builder's of Homes & Commercial Spaces

About Customer

This reputed real estate developer headquartered in Bengaluru, acclaimed for its commitment to delivering high- quality affordable housing. They have a significant presence in Bengaluru and other key cities in Karnataka, Kerala, and Tamil Nadu.

Business Challenge

They needed a robust digital solution to streamline their daily operations, manage communication effectively, and enhance customer engagement.



Approach

The company partnered with Superior Digital to integrate Apple technology into their workflow, focusing on empowering their Marketing representatives with tools for dynamic customer presentations.

Usage

Over 70 iPads were integrated for workflow. Marketing representatives use these iPads for customer presentations, showcasing 3D walkthroughs, and conducting Virtual Reality demonstrations. The deployment was managed with a Mobile Device Management (MDM) system, enabling seamless content distribution, policy enforcement, and remote support.



Outcomes

The adoption of iPads for immersive presentations resulted in improved operational efficiency and enhanced communication across various departments. This allowed the company to provide better service to customers and adapt quickly to market demands, reinforcing their reputation as a leader in the affordable housing sector.

Apple and Partner solutions demonstrated

Superior Digital provided over 17 MacBook Pros to a leading real estate and construction firm to empower their sales and customer service teams. The Macs are used to deliver stunning Keynote presentations and showcase 3D models and virtual walkthroughs, enhancing client engagement and project visualisation.
