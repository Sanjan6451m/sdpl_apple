What is TCO?

Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) measures the long-term costs of IT deployments, including hardware, software, support, and operations. A 2021 Forrester TEI study, commissioned by Apple, found Mac deployments deliver a 336% ROI and $101.3 million NPV for a composite organization with 100,000 employees deploying 33,000 Macs over three years. Forrester TEI Study

With M4 chip-enabled Macs, enterprises benefit from superior performance, energy efficiency, and battery life, reducing TCO by up to $843 per device compared to PCs, per Forrester and IBM data. Jamf Blog
